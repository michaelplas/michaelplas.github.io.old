But Why?

This website is solely created to simulate and inform the Next Generation of Phishing called -- Quishing! (QR Code Phishing)

Gone are the days of traditional email phishing attacks. This new threat allows attackers to create QR codes that appear legitimate, but tricking users into scanning them.

Once scanned, the QR code directs users to a malicious website under the attacker's control.

Ways to Prevent Quishing

Verify Source

If you receive a suspicious QR code in email, verify the sender's identity by checking their email address, name, and logo.

Preview URLs

Before accessing a QR code, use a QR app that provides a URL preview and ensure the URL is not suspicious.


Setup MFA

Enhance security with multi-factor authentication, adding an extra layer of protection through additional verification.

Inform Others

Spread awareness and protect your community by sharing this knowledge to prevent them from becoming victims
